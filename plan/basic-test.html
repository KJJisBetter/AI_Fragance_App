Fragrance Testing Guide

DAILY DRIVER BATTLE

Purpose: Everyday use when you need reliability
Examples: Work commute, gym, errands, casual lunch
Pick the TWO that feel most comfortable and versatile

COLLEGE/CAMPUS BATTLE

Purpose: For classroom, study sessions, and campus social life
Examples: Lectures, library sessions, campus events
Pick ONE that's pleasant, not distracting, and crowd-pleasing

SUMMER/WARM WEATHER BATTLE

Purpose: For hot, humid days when you need something refreshing
Examples: Beach days, outdoor activities, park outings
Pick ONE that feels most cooling and pleasant

OFFICE/PROFESSIONAL BATTLE

Purpose: For work settings requiring confidence and professionalism
Examples: Job interviews, business meetings, presentations
Pick ONE that feels sophisticated but not overpowering

CLUB/NIGHT OUT BATTLE

Purpose: For nightlife when you want to make an impression
Examples: Clubs, bars, concerts, parties
Pick ONE with the best projection and appeal

DATE NIGHT BATTLE

Purpose: For romantic settings when you want to create attraction
Examples: Dinner dates, intimate evenings
Pick ONE that feels most seductive and memorable

SIGNATURE SCENT BATTLE

Purpose: Your personal trademark that becomes identified with you
Examples: Everyday life when you want to be consistently recognized
Pick ONE that feels most "you" and versatile year-round

FALL/WINTER BATTLE

Purpose: For cold weather when you need something warmer
Examples: Holiday gatherings, cold days, winter activities
Pick ONE that feels cozy and substantial

SPECIAL OCCASION BATTLE

Purpose: For important events requiring sophistication
Examples: Weddings, formal dinners, milestone celebrations
Pick ONE that feels most refined and impressive

Testing Instructions

  1. For each category, have your friend label test strips A, B, C (and D if needed)
  2. Don't look at which fragrance is which - smell them blind
  3. Pick your favorite(s) for each category by checking the boxes
  4. After all testing is complete, have your friend reveal which fragrances you selected
  5. Use the "Save Results" button to save or share your selections

Building Your Collection

Start with:

  • 2 Daily Drivers (rotation)
  • 1 College/Campus fragrance
  • 1 each from the other categories you regularly encounter

This will give you a well-rounded collection covering all occasions without unnecessary duplication.
